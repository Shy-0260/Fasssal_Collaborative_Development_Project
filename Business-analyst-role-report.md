These are my findings on the role of a Business Analyst regarding the assignment. The group manager can review this information and place it into the appropriate boxes


Transport to and from the University
As a Business Analyst, I would gather data from students about transport challenges using surveys or interviews. I would analyse peak travel times and identify common delays. Based on the findings, I could recommend improvements such as better communication of transport schedules or a simple tracking system. The aim would be to improve punctuality and convenience for students.

Limited Support Issues
I would examine how the current support system operates and identify where delays occur. By analysing queue times and common requests, I could suggest improvements like an online appointment or ticketing system. This would help reduce waiting times and make support services more efficient.

Navigation of the Campus
I would evaluate how students move around campus and identify areas where confusion happens. A possible solution could be developing a digital campus map or improving signage. I would ensure the solution is easy to use and accessible for new students.

Cheap Events for Students
I would review attendance records and student feedback to understand which events are popular and affordable. Based on data analysis, I could suggest low-cost, high-impact activities to increase student engagement.

IT Issues (Canvas, Evision, etc.)
I would collect information on common technical problems and work with IT support to identify patterns. I could recommend clearer user guides or system improvements to reduce login and access issues.

Clubs and Societies
I would analyse participation levels and identify barriers preventing students from joining. I could propose better communication methods, such as a central online platform, to improve awareness and inclusion
